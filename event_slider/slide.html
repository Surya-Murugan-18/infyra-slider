Algorift
Crack the logic. Follow the trail. Unearth the final key
AlgoRift is a treasure hunt for coders where participants solve a series of technical clues testing programming, debugging, algorithms, cryptography, and logic. Each solved puzzle unlocks the next location or hint, guiding teams through a maze of digital and physical checkpoints. The first team to reach the final treasure wins. The event combines logic, speed, and teamwork.

RenderBlind
Design in the dark. Create with clarity
RenderBlind is a two-round UI/UX design showdown where visual creativity meets verbal clarity. It challenges participants to build user interfaces not only through design thinking but through communication, memory, and precise execution under constraints. This isn’t just another UI challenge it’s about how clearly you can explain design, how well your partner can interpret vision, and how perfectly you can recreate something you’ve barely seen.

Botforge
Code the mind. Create the voice
BotForge is a single-round technical sprint where participants build intelligent, domain-specific chatbots using AI or rule-based logic. In just 75 minutes, teams must design and implement a fully functional chatbot capable of handling real-world user interaction. This challenge blends coding, conversational design, and rapid problem-solving.

Crack the Capsule, Create the Future
Concept Capsule is a high-energy idea pitching challenge where participants don’t come prepared they draw their problem statement on the spot and must build an innovative solution using a provided template. Wildcards and time pressure will push their creativity, logic, and pitch skills to the limit!

Echelon Conflux
Crack the Code, Capture the Flag
CTF Showdown is an adrenaline-pumping cybersecurity competition where participants must think like real-world hackers to solve challenges. The game tests skills in problem-solving, cryptography, forensics, reverse engineering, web exploitation, and logical thinking. Every solved challenge reveals a hidden flag, and the team with the most points at the end reigns supreme.

Plea to Gaia
Convince the Divine. Save Our Future
In Plea to Gaia, each team of 3 to 5 members internally selects one person to embody Gaia, the Earth Goddess. The remaining members act as humans pleading for salvation from a specific environmental disaster. The humans must craft a compelling, original, and socially aware plea to convince Gaia to save their team and the planet. This immersive event blends persuasive communication, creativity, sustainability awareness, and strategic teamwork, with the unique twist that Gaia is part of the team adding depth to collaboration and decision-making.

Polymorphic Recall is a multi-round coding competition designed to rigorously test participants' ability to memorize, interpret, debug, and optimize code snippets across different languages and representations. This event challenges teams to master recursive logic, adapt to varied code paradigms, and optimize their memory recall under timed pressure
